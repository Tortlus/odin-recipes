Poppy Seed Chicken

Delicous poppy seed chicken!

A classic homestyle casserole. Easy to make and enjoyed by all!

  • 5 cups of chicken breast
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 21.25 ounces condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 2 cups crushed crackers
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1 tablespoon poppy seeds

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (177 C).
  2. Place shredded chicken in 9x13 casserole dish.
  3. Stir together the condensed soup and sour cream. Pour over the chicken.
  4. In a separate bowl, stir together crushed crackers and melted butter. Sprinkle over chicken and sauce. Add poppy seeds on top.
  5. Bake for 20-30 minutes. Or until top is browned and sauce is bubbly.
